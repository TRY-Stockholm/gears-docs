## What is Gears?

Gears is TRY Stockholm's production commerce toolkit (originally built when the team was Made People, before the agency joined TRY). It's a family of React packages — shipped under `@made-people/grease-*` and `@made-people/blueprint-*` — that handle the hard parts of running a modern headless storefront on top of Centra:

- Session-scoped **cart** state with optimistic updates
- Multi-PSP **checkout** flows (Adyen, Klarna, Qliro) with pluggable shipping widgets
- Product variant resolution, attribute mappers, PDP + PLP
- **Account + order history**, including passwordless identification via Voyado
- Faceted **search and recommendations** (Algolia)
- Headless **CMS** integration with live preview (Storyblok)
- **Feature flags and experiments** (GrowthBook)
- **Analytics** (Google Tag Manager) and shipping (Ingrid)

You compose Gears into a Remix app. You wire your configuration, stack the providers in the right order, register your routes, and the packages do the rest. Six live storefronts run on this stack today — [Stronger](https://www.strongerlabel.com), [Miss Mary](https://www.missmary.com), [Dedicated](https://www.dedicatedbrand.com), [Astrid Lindgren](https://www.astridlindgren.com), [Röhnisch](https://www.rohnisch.com), and [Mini Rodini](https://www.minirodini.com) — and every pattern in these docs is lifted from one of them.
